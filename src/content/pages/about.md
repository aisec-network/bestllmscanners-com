Best LLM Scanners compares LLM security scanning tools — Garak, PyRIT, promptmap, and the growing field of vendor-specific scanners — on coverage, detection rates, ease of use, and integration into CI/CD pipelines.

## What we compare

- **Open-source scanners** — Garak, PyRIT, promptmap, and community-maintained tools, compared on coverage and usability
- **Commercial scanners** — vendor LLM security scanning products, assessed on detection rates and operational overhead
- **Coverage gap analysis** — which attack categories each scanner misses and why
- **CI/CD integration** — pipeline integration complexity, output formats, and failure thresholds
- **Scanner output interpretation** — how to triage scanner output and prioritize findings

## Who reads this

Security engineers, MLSecOps practitioners, and developers who need to integrate LLM security scanning into their build and evaluation pipelines before attackers find the gaps.
